Notes from the Registrar

  • Pakistani universities (LUMS, IBA, NUST, FUUAST, NUML) weigh an aptitude test, SAT/ACT score, and interview alongside grades — there's rarely a single fixed cut-off. Check the current cycle's policy directly.
  • UK figures are typical offers, expressed as A-Level grade combinations (e.g. A*AA). They move slightly most years.
  • Where Part Two says a subject is "required," it's almost always still possible without it — just at a steeper grade bar. Further Maths usually makes the offer easier to hit rather than being the only door in.
  • This page is a research starting point, not a final shortlist. Re-verify every requirement on the linked, official page before you apply.
